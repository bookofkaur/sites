🎓 College Confused

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version

College Confused does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics trackers, and no ads. It's a free nonprofit tool, and your college journey is your business.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — application timelines, scholarship lists, essay drafts, test scores — is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) on your own device. It never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing browser data deletes it.

Children's privacy

College Confused is designed for students 13 and up. Because we collect nothing, there is no personal information from any user — of any age — on our servers. We don't have servers that store user data at all.
