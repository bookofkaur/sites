Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version

Sole Ops does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics trackers, and no ads.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — inventory, sales, listings, settings — is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) on your own device. It never leaves your device unless you export it yourself. Uninstalling the app or clearing browser data deletes it.

AI features

If you use the optional AI analysis features, the text you submit is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response, using an API key you provide. We never see this data. Anthropic's handling of API data is described in their privacy policy.

Payments

Paid plans are processed by Stripe. Your payment details go directly to Stripe and are never seen or stored by us. See Stripe's privacy policy.
